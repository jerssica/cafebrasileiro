home / about-us

From our home in Rio de Janeiro and our business base in New York City, our beverage company - Buy Brasil - will continue to expand its selection of products, services and its market penetration over the coming years. Buy Brasil is a producer of quality Brazilian beverages and we intend to market these beverages into the global marketplace. Our intention is to also bring our customers closer to the origin of our ideas and encourage buyers to get to know the product brand and the people who manage the brand. We believe there is far too much base commodity out there and not near enough of a personal connection to the niche origins of the product. The Buy Brasil client focus is hotels, restaurants, cocktail bars, holiday resorts and cafes. This is the preferred clientele that better suits our business profile. Our marketing will continue to reflect more about the niche qualities of Brazilian coffee and cachaça and the positive value of direct communication with its Brazilian producers. When it comes to Brazilian commodity and lifestyle products there’s a point worth noting. International consumers are intrigued, they don’t have to buy Brazilian products, they want to buy Brazilian products.

who we are

Gary Ridge - Manager, Business Director

A resident of Rio de Janeiro and an Australian national, Gary has a business history in music publishing and production. He has licensed and published music in all major international markets and his clients at one time included Carlos Santana Management for the year 2000 multi Grammy Award winning recording and DVD “A Supernatural Evening with Santana”.

On his arrival in Brazil as a permanent citizen in 2007, he established Gotalk-brasil an English language consultancy. His clientele include staff members from PetroBras, FGV, BNDES, Vale, ONS, Chemtech and L’Oreal.

Gary continues today to advise and ghost-write for a number of Brazilian professionals from the medical, legal and academic sectors.

Lorenzo McFarlane - Director of Marketing & Advertising

Lorenzo is a native New Yorker raised in Brooklyn. He commenced is career as an Assistant Manager for the Oceana Ballroom in Brighton Beach Brooklyn, one of New York City’s largest nightclubs. Lorenzo’s challenge was to build the business and he succeeded by organizing events outside the night life calendar, this to include weddings, award ceremonies, fund raisers to name a few. It was during one of Lorenzo’s customer events that he was introduced to Sath Nop, President of Sakiri Vodka. After a few drinks and a long marketing conversation they both recognized they shared a mutual interest in business development.

“Taking on Sakiri Vodka was definitely an experience; this was a time when New York City had an unlimited variety of Vodka so working it up to competitive level was difficult.”

He creating an innovative consignment sales plan for nightclub owners to market Sakiri Vodka, the idea was simple but no one was driving an alcoholic product in this manner. Lorenzo possesses a youthful business vision and brings attitude to Buy Brasil Inc to succeed in a marketplace full of new challenges and obstacles.
